Admission

Admission policy

AIM

The aim of this policy document is to promote and facilitate the admission of learners from any race to school. It reflects the democratic principles of the National Education Department to comply with its constitutional responsibility to ensure learners access to quality education

Who should Register

Learners who are currently registered at schools should in principle stay in same school and therefore do not need to register annually. Learners , who wish to attend a specific school for the first time, should register before the end of the revelent year for the beginning of the following year. All registrations should be completed by the tenth school day of the following year

Documentations Required For Admission

All documents including ID's, birth certificate, transfer cards, reports, etc, should be checked and verified carefully before admission to a school is affected

Age Of Learners

*Grade one: A learner should not be admitted to a primary school unless he or she should reach the age of six in the year of admission

*If the school holds the view that a learner is under the school age and no documentary proof of the contrary can be submitted, the principal may in consultation with the district office refuse the relevant learner admission to the school, while attention should be given to the official channels through which proper documentation can be obtained

Learners With Special Educational Needs

Learners with special educational needs (LSEN) should be admitted to the LSEN class only when they have been recommended for such class by an educational resource center, and only after and valid evaluation
